HOME

BORROW.COM is the startup that help individual to rent out their items.

Benefits

The BORROW apps has been helping millions of people to make money out of the items that is lying in their storeroom. The unique feature is that BORROW tracks the loan and provide the transction platform for borrower and lender to transact.

Belief

BORROW believes that lending items to one another reduce excess and waste in the society. It also hope to create a better society that people share and care, and make new friends through the transaction.

How to Borrow?

1. Upload your items that is available to lend to others.
Or submit a request of items you need to broow
2. Choose to borrow from around where your are, or from your FB Contacts only.
3. BORROW will broadcast your needs and link you up to a willing lender.
4. Seller may ask for a fee and duration of the loan
5. If both parties agreed to BORROW conditions. Transaction is made.
6. BORROW track the loan and remind parties when to return.
7. BORROW can help to arrange shipping service to both parties (with a fee)
