Professional Summary

A seasoned leader in large organisations with 20+ years’ experience in change management, systems thinking, learning and organisation development, product development and project management. Kam Yean has a unique combination of expertise in management, technology, operation, training design and has deep experiences working with governments. He completed an Executive MBA from the Singapore Management University in May 2017, and was certified by the Institution of Engineers Singapore (IES) as Chartered Engineer in Systems Engineering in Aug 2017. He has been a member of Skillsfuture Singapore's Training and Adult Education (TAE) Skills Council since 2014. One of his key accomplishments was leading the SAF's (Singapore Armed Forces’) Learning Transformation which saw the courses in 20 SAF training institutions become more learner-centric, leveraging mobile devices, enterprise IT, and e-learning content. Kam Yean is entrenched in a wide array of technologies, ranging from military equipment, weapon and command control systems, enterprise IT systems, Learning Portal, Learning Management System, data analytics, simulation and gaming technologies including virtual and augmented reality. Additionally, he has extensive experience working with procurement agencies, government agencies, foreign militaries, industries, engineers (hardware, software, human factor engineers), academia and consultants. His key strength is in the ability to marry up new technology with management to transform organizations.
